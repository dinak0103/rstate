How It Works

Our work flow is very simple.

Find Apartments for Rent

Featuring hundreds of thousands of apartments and houses for rent, R. State makes it easy for you to find your perfect home. Check prices, see photo displays, floor plans, amenities, and more!

Secure Leases

Secure your lease quickly and hassle-free, online or in-person. With R. State, you can check real-time availability, contact properties, submit rental applications, and receive approval notifications.

Rest Easy

It’s good to be home! On R. State you can make secure rent payments, view balances, submit maintenance requests, get community news, and renew your lease — all under one roof.
